DeepSeek Internal Architecture

An architectural teardown of how tokens route through DeepSeek's internal framework. When a prompt is entered, it bypasses standard dense compute layers; instead, the Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) system activates only specific neural experts suited for the request. Concurrently, Multi-head Latent Attention (MLA) optimizes the key-value cache memory footprint, drastically speeding up processing and inference generation for complex user prompts.

FAQFlow AI

FAQFlow AI is an AI-powered FAQ management and chatbot platform that enables businesses to upload their knowledge base and provide instant, accurate responses to customer queries. It uses Flask, SQLite, TF-IDF, and Google Gemini AI to deliver intelligent answers and offers an embeddable chatbot widget for seamless website integration.
